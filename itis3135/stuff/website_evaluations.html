Chill Snake || Website Evaluations

Good Website -- ebay

A proxy site to make ordering items from Japan easier.

    CRAP Principles followed:
  • Consistent grids
  • Good contrast

The ebay website displays good CRAP principles by having good contrast, consistent grids and consistent groupings.

Bad Website -- Buyee

A proxy site to make ordering items from Japan easier.

    CRAP Principles not followed:
  • Inconsistent grids
  • Inconsistent groupings

The Buyee website frequently displays poor following of the CRAP rules by including inconsistent grids and multiple NAV bars which link to the same things.
